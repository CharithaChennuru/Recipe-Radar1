Coconut Rice

Coconut rice is a dish made from rice cooked with coconut milk or coconut flakes, which gives it a rich and aromatic flavor

Mango Rice

Mango rice, also known as Mamidikaya Pulihora in Telugu cuisine, is a popular South Indian dish made with cooked rice, raw mango, and a blend of spices

Bisi Bille badh

Bisi bele bath is a traditional South Indian dish from Karnataka, known for its rich and flavorful preparation.
