Non-vegetarian Foods

Sushi and Sashimi

Sashimi is a Japanese dish consisting of thinly sliced raw fish or seafood, served without rice. It is typically accompanied by soy sauce and wasabi

Chicken Biriyani

Chicken biryani is a classic Indian dish that features spiced chicken and fragrant rice cooked together to create a flavorful and aromatic meal

Mutton Curry Puffs

Mutton curry cuffs, also known as Mutton Kofta Curry, are a flavorful dish from Indian cuisine.
