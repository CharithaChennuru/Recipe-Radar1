Ingredients

  • Lettuce (such as romaine, iceberg, or mixed greens)
  • Tomatoes, sliced or diced
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Carrots, grated or sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Avocado, sliced or diced (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (such as parsley, cilantro, or basil), chopped
  • Nuts or seeds (such as almonds, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, or sunflower seeds)
  • Feta cheese or goat cheese (optional, for added flavor)
  • Store-bought dressing
  • Homemade dressing made with olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, herbs, and spices

Process

  1. Wash all the vegetables and fruits thoroughly.
  2. Tear or chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large salad bowl.
  3. Add the sliced or diced tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, carrots, and red onion to the bowl.
  4. If using avocado, slice or dice it and add it to the salad.
  5. Sprinkle fresh herbs, nuts, and seeds over the vegetables.
  6. If using cheese, crumble it over the salad.
  7. Drizzle the dressing over the salad, starting with a small amount and adding more as needed. Toss the salad gently to coat the ingredients evenly with the dressing.
  8. Taste the salad and adjust the seasoning or dressing according to your preference.
  9. Serve the fresh salad immediately as a side dish or as a light meal on its own.
